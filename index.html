Courtney Pobst is a second year at the University of California, Riverside. She is studying Theatre, Film and Digital Production, as well as Creative Writing.

Her end goal is to someday work as a Production Stage Manager for a theatre company, and also be a sought after writer for movies and television shows. Courtney has volunteered for major organizations all of her life, such as Susan G. Komen Race for the Cure, the Alzheimer's Association, the Make-A-Wish foundation, the SPCA, and the Rancho Buena Vista Library annually.

Courtney has taken a plethora of creative writing, film, and theatre classes that gave her hands on experiences in the industry starting at the age of sixteen and her love for the arts has only flourished more since then. She is also a part of many wonderful programs and organizations, such as the National Honors Society, Phi Sigma Pi Honors Fraternity, Gamma Beta Phi Honors Society, served as a member of ASB and Link Crew in her high school, organized the North County High School Film Festival in 2018, and is currently working as a scenic carpenter, painter, and properties assistant manager at the theatre department at UCR.
